Baseline Architecture

The baseline architecture of the Cathrince Printing Shop represents its current state, which includes existing resources, capabilities, and operations. In this stage, your printing shop operates with limited services and equipment, making it challenging to meet customer standards. The focus is on addressing foundational aspects to ensure operational efficiency, customer satisfaction, and financial stability.

Target Architecture

The target architecture of the printing shop represents an ambitious vision for the future of the business. With a budget of 500,000 pesos, this vision is centered on transforming the printing shop from its current baseline architecture into a thriving and diversified enterprise. It involves a comprehensive expansion of services and capabilities, enable to meet and exceed higher customer standards. The shop is poised to offer a wide range of advanced printing services, including large-format printing, custom packaging printing, and specialized printing solutions. The target architecture reflect dedication to achieving a leadership position in the printing industry, providing innovative, high-quality printing solutions to a diverse customer base.
